## Visual direction

BULWARK is a multi-faction, multi-domain tower defense presented in a layered 2.5D style — stacked 2D sprites faked into depth via layer order, shadows, and camera rotation. The look is clean and legible with readable combat telegraphing (sensor→weapon), a signature gold 'pie-sweep' completion flash, and a select→preview→confirm deploy loop that works identically on mouse or a single finger. Nine asymmetric pop-culture-trope factions battle across ground, water, and air terrain.
